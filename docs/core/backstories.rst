Fatebearer
======

**Ratings modifier**; pick one:

- no modifier
- Might +1, Wyrd -1
- Vigor +1, Poise -1
- Poise +1, Might -1
- Ego +1, Reason -1
- Reason +1, Vigor -1
- Wyrd +1, Ego -1

**Pick one of these moves:**

.. card:: Sneak Peek

    When the Narrator begins a new chapter, roll +Wyrd to **have a hunch about your near future**. On a 10+, the Narrator will offer you some valuable insight. On a 7-9 you get a vague hint. On a miss, anything goes.

.. card:: Stubborn Prophecy

    There’s something you are destined to do. Work out the details with the Narrator, based on your fate. You cannot die until it comes to pass. **If you die in play**, you must spend a Fate. You will then, somehow, recover or be returned to life. Once your task is done or you use up all your Fates, all bets are off.

**... then another of these moves:**

- (TBW)
- **Dutiful**: When your fate rears its ugly head, and **you act in accordance with any of your fate tags** (either heroic or doom) earn 1 EXP. If it’s a heroic tag, take +1 forward.
- **Warrior of Legend**: You have a special artifact you are destined to wield - a weapon, magic focus, or tool of some kind. Talk with the Narrator about what it should be, what tags it should have, and how it has and will fit into your story. Your artifact will always return to you after some time.
