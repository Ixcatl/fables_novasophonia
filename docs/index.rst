Fables of Nova Sophonia
*******

In an unfamiliar and far removed cosmos spins the marble that is Nova Sophonia: a world of unbridled magic, incredible feats and terrible secrets. Common folk mingle freely with the realms beyond all while Gods dream among the stars. Here, even the lowest coward may find himself wound in a fate far greater than he could ever imagine.

Nova Sophonia is a realm of great majesty and potential, sure, but to say it's without danger in equal measure would be plainly stupid. (TBW TBW TBW)

The stories of these entangled cultures tell of their heroes small and large, pious and reserved, mighty and meek. A noble drake, towering quadrupedal warrior of some legendary battle, may very well command as much respect as the smallest ratling hedge magus, or the average folk hero not entirely under the law. No matter the people, anyone has their own side of things they'd be more than eager to share.
